## Our Pledge

We pledge to foster an open, respectful, and ethically grounded community.

AIC-Energy is not just a technical project â€” it is a moral and systemic experiment.  
Behavior within this community matters as much as the code.

---

## Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

- Engage with honesty and intellectual humility
- Respect differing perspectives and backgrounds
- Critique ideas rigorously without personal attacks
- Be mindful of power, hierarchy, and implicit coercion
- Prioritize long-term collective good over individual gain

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following will not be tolerated:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Weaponizing technical knowledge against others
- Gaslighting, psychological manipulation, or bad-faith debate
- Promotion of authoritarian, exploitative, or dehumanizing ideologies
- Attempts to redirect the project toward surveillance, control, or profit-maximization agendas

---

## Ethical Boundary

AIC-Energy explicitly rejects:

- Use for mass surveillance
- Military or offensive cyber operations
- Energy manipulation for political coercion
- Exploitative extraction of human or environmental resources

Any advocacy for these directions will result in removal.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to:

- Remove content
- Reject contributions
- Suspend or ban participants

Decisions will be explained transparently when possible.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it discreetly to the maintainers.

Reports will be handled with seriousness, confidentiality, and care.

---

This Code of Conduct exists to protect **people, principles, and the future**.
